# Discovery

Your personalized home for new music, recommendations, and curated content.

## Accessing Discovery

Click **Discover** in the sidebar or press `Cmd/Ctrl + D`.

## Discovery Sections

The Discovery page displays various sections based on your listening history and preferences.

### Quick Picks

Instantly generated recommendations based on recent listening.

- Updates every session
- Mix of familiar and new
- Click to play, right-click for options

### New Releases

Recently released music matching your taste.

- Updated daily
- Filtered to your genre preferences
- Shows release date

### Trending Now

Popular tracks across the platform.

- Most played this week
- Regional trending (if available)
- Viral tracks

### Because You Like...

Similar artists and tracks based on your favorites.

- Generates multiple "Because you like [Artist]" rows
- Click artist name to explore more
- Learns from your interactions

### Weekly Rotation

A personalized mix refreshed every week.

- Generated every Monday
- 25-50 track playlist
- Balances discovery and familiarity

### Artist Radio

Endless mix based on an artist you love.

- Click "Start Radio" on any artist
- Mixes similar artists
- Never runs out

### Mood Mixes

Music for different moments:

- **Focus** - Concentration-friendly tracks
- **Workout** - High-energy music
- **Chill** - Relaxed vibes
- **Party** - Upbeat hits

### Genre Explorer

Browse music by genre:

- All major genres
- Subgenre drill-down
- Cross-genre discovery

### Decade Mixes

Music from different eras:

- Best of 2020s
- 2010s Hits
- 2000s Throwbacks
- Classic collections

## Interacting with Discovery

### Playing Content

- **Click** track to play
- **Click** album/playlist to view
- **Hover** for quick play button
- **Double-click** to play immediately

### Context Menu

Right-click any item for options:

- Add to Queue
- Add to Playlist
- Like/Dislike
- Go to Artist
- Go to Album
- Share

### Refreshing

- Pull down (or click refresh) to get new recommendations
- Sections update based on time of day
- Login to sync preferences

## Personalization

### Training Your Taste

The more you interact, the better recommendations become:

1. **Like tracks** you enjoy (heart icon)
2. **Dislike tracks** you don't want (X icon)
3. **Skip tracks** you're not interested in
4. **Complete tracks** to signal enjoyment
5. **Create playlists** to define preferences

### Factors Considered

| Factor | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Liked tracks | High |
| Completed plays | High |
| Skipped tracks | Medium |
| Playlist additions | Medium |
| Time of day | Low |
| Day of week | Low |

### Discovery Settings

Configure in **Settings** > **Discovery**:

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Show Explicit | Include explicit content |
| Discovery Radius | How adventurous recommendations are |
| Blocked Artists | Artists to never recommend |
| Favorite Genres | Priority genres |

## Discovery Features

### Time-Aware Recommendations

Audiio adjusts suggestions based on time:

- **Morning**: Upbeat, energizing music
- **Afternoon**: Focused, productive vibes
- **Evening**: Relaxed, unwinding tracks
- **Night**: Calm, sleep-friendly options

### Listening Streaks

Track your discovery habits:

- Days in a row you've explored new music
- New artists discovered this week
- Genre exploration breadth

### Discovery Weekly Email

Optional weekly email with:

- Your top new discoveries
- Recommended releases
- Personalized picks

Enable in **Settings** > **Notifications**.

## Tips for Better Discovery

### Explore Different Times

Check Discovery at different times of day for varied recommendations.

### Use Radio Feature

Start artist radios to discover similar music.

### Rate Consistently

The more you like/dislike, the more accurate suggestions become.

### Try Mood Mixes

Even if you don't usually listen to that mood, you might discover new favorites.

### Check Weekly Rotation

Don't miss the weekly refresh - it's curated just for you.

## Troubleshooting

### Recommendations Seem Wrong

1. Like/dislike more tracks
2. Check blocked artists list
3. Clear discovery cache in Settings
4. Use for a few more days

### No New Content

1. Pull to refresh
2. Check internet connection
3. Try different sections
4. Recommendations update periodically

### Missing Sections

Some sections require:
- Enough listening history
- Specific addons installed
- Account features enabled
